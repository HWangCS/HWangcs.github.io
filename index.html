About me

I am a postdoc associate in the Department of Computer Science at Yale University, where I work with Prof. Robert Soule and Prof. Avi Silberschatz.

I received my Ph.D. at University of Virginia and advised by Prof. Helen Shen. Also, I received my B.S. at University of Science & Technology of China (USTC) and M.S. at Columbia University. I am very fortunate to work with a number of wonderful people.

Research

My research interests include Cloud Computing, Datacenter Network, Distributed System, Machine Learning, and Renewable Energy.
