About me

I am a researcher in the DSSS department at NEC Labs America.

I am a postdoc associate in the Department of Computer Science at Yale University, where I work with Prof. Robert Soule and Prof. Avi Silberschatz.

I received my Ph.D. at University of Virginia, advised by Prof. Helen Shen. Also, I received my B.S. at University of Science & Technology of China (USTC) and M.S. at Columbia University. I am very fortunate to work with a number of wonderful people.

Research

I'm interested in AI Agent, LLM, Cloud and Distributed systems, especially in Multi Agent System, LLM Agent, Reinforcement Learning, Simulation, Distributed Systems and Datacenter Networks.
